2.Source spectrum calculated by EGF method and analysis of the spectra

Advisor: Prof. Sidao Ni, Prof. Yunyi Qian

South California is active and earthquakes happen frequently. In 2019, there were two earthquakes happened within 1 day with magnitude of M6.4 and M7.1.

figure 2.1 Distribution of earthquakes and stations

Some earthquakes’ ruptures can stop without any barriers and are called the self-arresting rupture, while others cannot stop automatically and are called the runaway rupture. The latter one can cause larger damage to the society.

figure 2.2 Modeling of self-arresting and runaway rupture

Used the empirical Green’s function (EGF) method to calculate the apparent source spectra of 219 target events totally.

figure 2.3 Schematic diagram of EGF method

The source spectra of self-arresting and runaway ruptures can be distinguished by the features around the corner frequency(fc). There will be a “hole” around the fc in the source spectra of the runaway runture. However, the spectra of the self-arresting rupture are relatively smooth around fc.

figure 2.4 Theoretical source spectra of self-arresting and runaway rupture

The feature is obvious in the spectra in Ridgecrest.

figure 2.6 Observational source spectra of self-arresting rupture
